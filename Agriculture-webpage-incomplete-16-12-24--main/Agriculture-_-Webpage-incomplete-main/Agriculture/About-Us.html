Food Corporation of India (FCI)

The Food Corporation of India (FCI) is a statutory body established and run by the Government of India. It plays a key role in ensuring food security and supports the public distribution system (PDS) by purchasing food grains from farmers at minimum support prices and distributing them to the public.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To ensure that food security is achieved in India by providing food at affordable prices and ensuring its availability through an efficient public distribution system.

Vision: To become a leading organization in the world in terms of food security, providing support to farmers and consumers, while improving the food supply chain to ensure sustainability and food safety.
